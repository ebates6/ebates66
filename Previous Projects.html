Previous Website Projects

In my current position with FMCC, I manage a group of independent contractors that provide onsite support at various dealerships. The contractors are not employees of FMCC
and do not have VPN access to the companiesinternal network. I quickly noticed that I was sending repeatative emails such as contact info due to personnel changes; procedural
guidelines and expense reports to other Business Center's because they didn't have access to my business center's shared drive or because the contractors didn't have access to
internal sites.

As a solution, I created a peer-to-peer or B2B SharePoint site that the contractors could view without VPN access. I also utilized the site to store the administrative information that
was previous housed on a shared drive. Once I constructed the site, all I had to do was share the URL with the contractors and they could access the majority of information they
needed. I also included FAQ's on how to troublshoot their equipment and links to the various procedures they may need while onsite. I also added a library to store the invoices
and expense reports that were previously stored on a shared drive. This allowed the various Business Centers to access the expenses anytime they needed it and without having to
request it via email; as long as they had permission to view the site, they could access the information they needed. As a added security measure, I created groups and library
permission to ensure that the contractors couldn't access confidential information.
